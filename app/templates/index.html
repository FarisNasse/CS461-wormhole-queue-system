About The Wormhole

The Wormhole (Valley Library and Remote) is a Physics Collaboration and Help Center. It is an informal space to work on physics with your peers under the guidance of experienced Assistants.

If your group needs help with a physics question, send a request to the queue. Only one person in the group needs to enter the request. You can check your position in the help queue using the “Live Queue” link above.

Wormhole Assistants

Wormhole Assistants are graduate students in physics, physics majors, or other undergraduates who completed the introductory physics sequence. They can guide you, but may refer you to another Assistant or instructor.

Virtual Wormhole

The Virtual Wormhole allows remote help through:

  • The Introductory Physics MS Teams channel.
  • The Wormhole Zoom session here (password: Wormhole), with breakout rooms by course.

Requesting Assistance

In person: use the physical box on your table. Online: submit a request using the appropriate link. Help is provided in order received.

Code of Conduct

The Wormhole works best when we follow common courtesy.

  • Be respectful of other students.
  • Respect personal and work spaces.
  • Be mindful of aromas.
  • Be considerate of your speech.
  • Respect others using the space after you.
  • Be patient — Assistants help as many people as possible.
  • The Wormhole gets crowded. If you’re not working on physics, please give up your space.
