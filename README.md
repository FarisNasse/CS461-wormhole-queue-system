# The Wormhole Tutoring Center Web Center
A queue managment system used by the OSU physics tutoring center ("The Wormhole") to manage student help reuests both in-person and online. By combining web-based help and a network of button boxes, our system streamlines the tutoring process. It's easier than raising a hand for students and it allows admins to track how students are getting help.

## How it works
* Help request tickets get submitted by students
* Wormhole assistants log in to their accounts and manage the queue of tickets as they provide tutoring
* Admins monitor activity and analyze archives of tickets
